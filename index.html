Concept

Sometimes the term "chatbot" is used to refer to virtual voice assistants generally or specifically accessed by online chat. In some cases, online chat programs are only for entertainment purposes. With the development of artificial intelligence technology, people are no longer satisfied that some virtual voice assistants have only fun use instead of other practical uses.

My project is aiming to develop a new virtual voice assistant system focusing on the function of companionship. As it can be customized to meet different users, this system can be used in different areas of day-to-day life such as a digital butler, personal healthcare management, and even a digital friend or companion. When it is functioning its voice recognition system, it not only responds accurate information, but it also shows empathy and provides emotional wellbeing support.

Definition of Virtual Assistant

According to Wikipedia, an intelligent virtual assistant (IVA) or intelligent personal assistant (IPA) is a software agent that can perform tasks or services for an individual based on commands or questions. Some virtual assistants are able to interpret human speech and respond via synthesized voices. Users can ask their assistants questions, control home automation devices and media playback via voice, and manage other basic tasks such as email, to-do lists, and calendars with verbal commands. The assistant uses voice queries and a natural-language user interface to answer questions, make recommendations, and perform actions by delegating requests to a set of Internet services.



Function

Aura virtual voice assistant having boast broad compatibility can give you seamless control over disparate technology. It can complete household commands and monitor the comfort and security of your home base on the Internet of Things environment.

Through a smartphone and other wearable devices, Aura can monitor users’ healthcare and emotional wellbeing. While Aura detects the user’s whatever physical or mental issues, the system will respond immediately and provide health advice. If Aura detects that user comes across any emergency, it will contact local hospital, police or fire station automatically.

Showing empathy is another important feature. Users can have a more human conversation with Aura, just like to treat a friend. Based on the voice recognition system and big data analysis, the system can understand and respond more accurately. Aura is a good companion. Users can talk with Aura whenever they want to. As Aur has more conversation with users, Aur will develop and more understand users.

Her (film)

In a near future, lonely Theodore works writing beautiful handwritten letters in a company, and is divorcing from his wife Catherine. In his leisure time, he usually recalls the good moments of his life with Catherine and likes to play a new videogame. His best friend is his neighbor Amy, who is married to Charles. One day, Theodore sees the advertisement of a new operating system called OS-1 that is announced as the first artificially intelligent operating system and he decides to buy it. After the installation, he has a conversation with a seductive female voice and when he asks her name, she tells that she is Samantha. Soon Samantha develops her feelings and they fall in love with each other. The insecure Theodore feels divided for loving a computer system while Samantha does not stop to grow-up and evolve.

Customization

Aura is designed for everyone for different purposes. Users will be asked to complete a customized service requirement. Following this service requirement, Aura will generate a default system to meet these users.

For children and adolescents, Aura more focuses on safety and guardian services.
For adults, Aura more focuses on wellbeing and counseling services.
For elders, Aura more focuses on healthcare service.
