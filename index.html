Internet Infrastructure in Karori Rd, Wellington

Jim Guo





As the need of social development, Internet infrastructure is becoming more important for supporting residents’ daily life. Karori is one of New Zealand's biggest suburbs located at the western edge of Wellington. With a population of over 14,000 settle down here, some Internet infrastructures can be found along on its main road, Karori road.

Antenna

Most antennas are set for residents to transmit or receive the video and audio signals of television broadcasts. These antennas are mounted on a mast on top of the owner's house.
